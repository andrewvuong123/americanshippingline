What we can do

We have been very active in the freight business for more than a decade with excellent relationships among major airlines as well as ocean shipping lines. We can always secure spaces for our customers and guarantee professional, prompt, and personalized services. We can arrange your cargos to be picked up and assure the safe arrival of all cargos at their destinations.

Through our networks and agents' efforts, we can offer unique services for the following items:

1. Co-ordinating air or ocean cargo booking and tracking
2. Providing local pick-up, delivery and interstate trucking
3. Consolidating both air and ocean to and from anywhere in five continents
4. Preparing neccesary documentation such as the bank letter of credit
5. Checking with overseas for cargo deliveries to CNEE
6. Assisting customers through the proper paperwork and legal processes to meet the rules and regulations of the custom
7. Supplying insurance policies and claims when necessary
